Coffe Jelly That Even Saiki Kusuo Would Love

    Ingredients
      Gelatin
      Instant Coffee
      Sugar
      Water
      Whipped Cream or Ice Cream ( both optional)
    Instructions
  1. In a bowl brew the coffee by mixing coffee powder, sugar and hot water together.
  2. In another bowl place the gelatin and slowly pour the coffee mixture and mixing immediately until its combined well.
  3. Pour the combined mixture into a dessert cup.
  4. Regrigerate for 4 hours.
  5. Top coffee jelly with whipped cream or ice cream (optional).
