Onigiri aka Brock's Jelly Doughnuts from Pokemon

    Ingredients
      Kelp
      Water
      Rice Wine Vinegar
      Soy Sauce
      Mirin
      Sake
      Seasame Seeds
      Sour Pickled Japanese Plums
      Rice
      Sugar
      Salt
      Seaweed
    Steps
  1. Start by getting the sour pickled Japanese plums and mash them up, remove the pits of the pulm before doing so.
  2. Wash and clean the rice of the starch before placing into a rice cooker.
  3. Once the rice is ready spread the rice in any container or sheet pan and drizzle 2 Tbsp of rice wine vinegar, 1 Tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp of salt. Then mix.
  4. To form the Onigiri, start by wetting your hands so the rice won't stick to them. Then get a good portion of rice, shaping the rice into a disc as big as the size of a hockey puck.
  5. Place the filling, the sour pickled plums (or for Brock's Jelly Douchnut version put jam), into the center and fold the sides into a triangle.
  6. Serve
